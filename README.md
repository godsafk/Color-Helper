PAINT MIX HELPER V14

NEW — AUTO PICK COLORS

- Choose 8, 10, 12, 14, 16, or 20 colors.
- Click Auto Pick Colors.
- The app scans the entire image and finds large, visually distinct color regions.
- It avoids tiny isolated details and tries to place each number in a representative area.
- Colors are initially numbered from upper-left toward lower-right.
- You can still drag cards to reorder them or delete/add colors manually.
- Running Auto Pick again replaces the existing samples after confirmation.

The existing two-page export remains:
Page 1: sample-dot placement map.
Page 2: color-mix chart.

Extract the ZIP and open index.html directly in Chrome or Edge.
